# Spin Up a Persona-Diverse Agent Debate Panel for Unbiased Decisions

## TL;DR

For a decision (not code), create an agent team whose members hold distinct personas — skeptic, customer, competitor, domain expert — have each research independently, then make them debate until they reach consensus or name their core disagreement.

## Why it matters

A single Claude instance tends toward sycophancy and won't genuinely push back on your idea. Independent agents arguing from assigned vantage points surface real trade-offs and failure modes you'd otherwise miss, at a fraction of the effort of convening real reviewers.

## How to apply

Spin up 4-6 teammates, each with a one-line persona and its own research pass; then instruct them to debate ('keep going until you reach consensus or can state your core disagreement'). Read the debate transcript for the actionable tensions rather than just the final verdict.
